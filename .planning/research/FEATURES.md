# Features Research

## Table Stakes (Must Have)
- **Character Minting & Storage**: Full on-chain representation of stats and inventory.
- **Trustless Dice Rolling**: Verifiable Random Function (VRF) integration.
- **Scenario Management**: Creating, joining, and resolving game sessions.
- **Escrow/Staking**: Financial guarantees against player/GM abandonment.

## Differentiators
- **Dual-Staking Model**: Both KP and Players have financial skin in the game.
- **Deflationary Tokenomics (Token Sink)**: "Tearing the card" (character death) permanently burns assets/tokens.
- **Dual-Dimensional Reputation**: Separate tracking for Player and Keeper history.
- **Immersive VTT**: 1920s horror thematic UI directly tied to on-chain state changes (e.g., visual Sanity drop effects).

## Anti-Features (Do Not Build)
- **P2P Item Trading**: Unrestricted trading breaks scenario balance. Items must be bound or heavily restricted.
- **Full Voice/Text Chat on-chain**: Too expensive and slow. Defer to Discord.
